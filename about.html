About Me

Hi, I’m Kieni Edojah — a Business Systems Analyst with a background in front-end development and a growing passion for backend technologies and database management.

My journey into tech started when I launched my knitwear business, House of Kieni's Fashion. I wanted to build a website for it, so I tried no-code tools at first. But I quickly realized I wanted more control and creativity — so I began learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. That first project sparked a deeper interest in software development, and I’ve been building ever since.

To strengthen my skills, I earned certifications in Technical Systems Analysis and Operations Management, where I learned how to analyze workflows, document business requirements, and improve systems — all of which led to my current role as a Business Systems Analyst.

Today, I combine creative problem-solving with technical know-how to deliver real-world solutions. While I still enjoy front-end work, I’m now diving into backend development, SQL, and database design to become a more well-rounded tech professional.

Whether it's building responsive web pages, mapping out system flows, or helping businesses turn ideas into action — I bring curiosity, clarity, and a drive to learn and grow.
