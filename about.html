About Me

Hi, I’m Kieni Edojah, a passionate Front-End Developer with a strong foundation in building responsive, user-friendly websites that blend functionality with aesthetics. My journey into front-end development began with a simple yet transformative goal: to create a website for my business, House of Kieni's Fashion.

After earning my Bachelor’s in Electrical/Electronic Engineering, I embraced my creative side by starting a small knitwear manufacturing business. I wanted to expand its reach, so I decided to create a website. I started with no-code tools, but soon realized the potential of custom development and began learning programming to fully tailor my digital creations. This journey of discovery led me to pursue advanced certifications in Technical Systems Analysis and Operations Management, where I honed my analytical and problem-solving skills. I gained a deeper understanding of system workflows and learned how to transform ideas into seamless digital solutions.

I have developed strong skills in tools and technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Sass. While I’ve learned the fundamentals of Sass, I am eager to apply it in real-world projects. I am also actively learning React.js to expand my expertise and take on more dynamic and interactive development challenges. My goal is always to deliver exceptional user experiences and solve real-world problems.
